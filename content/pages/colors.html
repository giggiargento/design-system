Colors

Color distinguishes our brand and helps us create consistent experiences across products.

The color system is designed to meet the AA standard contrast ratios of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines. This makes things easier to find, identify, and interact with. It also makes the entire experience more accessible to users who are colorblind or have low vision.

In the case of Appoint Customers we allow customers to submit their own color palettes. Our product adapts intelligently and flexibly to meet the different needs and preferences of our customers.

Colors have assigned roles that have a specific meaning based on how they function within the interface.
Defined color roles make it easy to modify and customize later.

Secondary palette
The secondary or semantic palette is made up of green, yellow and red. Semantic color helps users find people, identify states, view actions, locate help, and understand next steps. The consistent use of color keeps the cognitive load low and creates a unified and engaging user experience.
