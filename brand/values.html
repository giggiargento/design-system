Values

As a team, we keep our standards high and inspire others to achieve the extraordinary.

Craft with love
Passion, urgency and need drive everything we do, but with love and wisdom we carefully consider every option before crafting.
Build health tools
Working for the health system requires specific knowledge in the area and to commit and gain trust with each user, doctor and patient in order to make each decision-making simpler, more efficient and safer.
Makes users happier
That our users feel comfortable and understood is our main objective, but make them a little happier, is our success.
Time is precious
We understand that time is the most valuable thing, therefore we always take into account how to optimize it when working.
A lifestyle
Work will always be work and will take time and dedication, therefore we choose to make work part of our day, as a lifestyle, working to live better but not living to work.
