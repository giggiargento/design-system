Mission

Bukeala is based on Panelita visual design language principles and helps designers and developers maintain brand consistency across projects and design assets.

Our mission is to contribute to carrying out a design system aimed at increasing health systems information management and be part of the technologies of the future providing our users with a new way to facilitate the optimization of time and processes.

We want to be one of the first teams to share, through open source, a design system with health as the north.
