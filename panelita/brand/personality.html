Personality

Our personality describes the essence we use when communicating visual information. We express how we are visually faithful to our mission, promise and values.

Clean
As our design system is focused on the construction of health software, we believe we must use white backgrounds and clean spaces.
Unsaturated
We seek to make a healthy environment something positive, avoiding saturated tones. We don't want to tire users but rather raise awareness at the present moment to not rush in making decisions.
Simple
It is all about simplicity. We creatively play with space, objects, illustrations, and texts to generate trust and rapprochement with others.
Universal
We use universal assets as elements, components, patterns, and code. All of our assets are considered universal which means they can be easily recognized and used.
